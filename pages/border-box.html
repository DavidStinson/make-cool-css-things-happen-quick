box-sizing: border-box vs. box-sizing: content-box

Typical Preferred Behavior

#borderBox {
  box-sizing: border-box;
  background-color: whitesmoke;
  height: 200px;
  width: 200px;
  padding: 35px;
  margin: 20px;
  border: 15px black solid;
}
